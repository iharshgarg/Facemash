Child Safety Standards

Facemash is committed to maintaining a safe and respectful environment for all users. We do not tolerate any form of child sexual abuse, exploitation, or harmful content involving minors.

Zero Tolerance Policy

Content that involves or promotes child sexual abuse or exploitation (CSAE) is strictly prohibited on Facemash. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Sexual content involving minors
  • Grooming, solicitation, or exploitation of children
  • Sharing, requesting, or distributing CSAM

User Reporting & Moderation

Users are encouraged to report any content or behavior that may violate our child safety standards. Reports are reviewed promptly, and appropriate action is taken, including content removal and account suspension where necessary.

Legal Compliance & Reporting

Facemash complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations. Where required, we cooperate with regional and national authorities and report confirmed violations in accordance with legal obligations.

Age Restrictions

Facemash is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly allow accounts belonging to underage users. If we become aware of such an account, it will be removed.
